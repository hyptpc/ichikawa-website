About the HypTPC Subgroup

Our group aims to develop next-generation researchers in nuclear and hadron physics, with graduate students taking the leading role in experiments and data analysis.

Through HypTPC experiments at J-PARC (E42, E45, E72, E90, E104), internal lab meetings, and participation in domestic and international conferences, students engage in hands-on research activities.

The HypTPC experiments are conducted in collaboration with major Japanese institutions such as JAEA-ASRC, KEK, Osaka University (RCNP), and Kyoto University.

We also collaborate with international universities such as Korea University and Kyungpook National University in South Korea, and Lamar University in the United States.
