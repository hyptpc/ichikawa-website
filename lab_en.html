About HypTPC Subgroup

Our group aims to cultivate researchers who excel at the forefront of nuclear and hadron physics. Graduate students play a leading role in both experiments and data analysis.

We collaborate with major research institutions in Japan (JAEA, KEK, RCNP, Kyoto Univ.) and international partners in Korea, the USA, and Germany.
